# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel to analyze business performance across products, customers, geography, and payment behavior.

The dashboard converts raw transactional data into meaningful insights through interactive slicers, KPI tracking, and insight-driven visualizations.

---

## 📁 Project Structure
-  **Datasets/** – Raw data used for analysis  
- **Excel_Dashboard/** – Final dashboard file  
- **Images/** – Dashboard screenshots  
 

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- **KPI Dashboard** - Net Sales, Transactions, Average Sales, and Product Quantity
- **Sales Analysis** – Category-wise performance with Net vs Gross comparison  
- **Customer Segmentation** – Sales distribution by age group  
- **Product Insights** – Quantity sold and discount impact analysis
- **Payment Analysis** – Comparison of transaction methods 
- **Geographic Insights** – Country-level sales comparison highlighting top markets 

---

## 🎛 Filters (Slicers)
- Month 
- Product Category  
- Product  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- USA leads total sales across countries  
- Credit cards drive the majority of transactions
- Coffee and Tea are the top-performing categories 
- Adult customers contribute the highest revenue share
